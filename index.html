BIO

Chirou S. Pabadora is a passionate filmmaker, cinematographer, and photographer based in North Caloocan City, Philippines. With experience spanning from junior high school to professional freelancing, he has worked on various creative projects showcasing his innovative storytelling and technical skills. A graduate of the Eugenio Lopez Jr. Center for Media Arts Senior High School and currently enrolled in the University of the Cordilleras under College of Information Technology and Computer Science, pursuing a Bachelor of Multimedia Arts.
